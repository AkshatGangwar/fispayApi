The Idea
----------
Our Innovative idea is to make cash easily available to the user for their daily needs. it is an effort to make cash available through cardless ATM(Digitally) and overcome the problem of waiting in long queue just to withdraw the money.

How it will work?
-----------------------
-Users and Store Owners will register and login to "FPAY" .
-User will add money to their FPAY wallet.
-By using map feature user will search for nearest store and will physically visit the store of their choice.
-Now user can transfer money from the wallet to store owner's account.
-In turn the store owner will provide the required cash to user.
